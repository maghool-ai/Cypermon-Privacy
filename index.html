Privacy Policy – Cypermon

Last updated: [05/12/2025]

Cypermon (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is a mobile game developed for entertainment purposes. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Cypermon app.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

We use Google Firebase services such as Firebase Authentication and Firebase Database. We may collect the following:

  • Email address
  • Display name or nickname
  • Firebase User ID (UID)

This information is used to create and manage your account and save your game progress.

1.2 Gameplay Data

We store gameplay progress including:

  • Levels completed
  • Items and achievements
  • In-game preferences

1.3 Usage and Analytics Data

Through Firebase Analytics, we may collect:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Region (not precise location)
  • App version
  • Interaction events (screens opened, actions, play duration)

1.4 Crash and Performance Data

If Firebase Crashlytics is enabled, we may collect crash logs and performance metrics to help improve stability and fix issues.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate the game and online features
  • To save and restore your game progress
  • To improve gameplay and overall experience
  • To fix bugs and technical issues
  • To protect game security

We do not sell your data to any third party.

3. Third-Party Services

Cypermon may use the following Google Firebase services:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Firestore / Realtime Database
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

These services collect data to help operate and improve the game.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your Cypermon account is active. If you request deletion, we will delete or anonymize your data within a reasonable time unless legally required otherwise.

5. Children's Privacy

Cypermon is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age. Please contact us if you believe a child has provided such data.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access, modify, delete, or object to the processing of your data. To request such actions, contact us using the email below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page and the "Last updated" date will change.
